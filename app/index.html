About our company

It is Kneller Design’s objective to offer a wide range of services to Facilities Managers and Commercial furniture suppliers, supported by modern CAD systems, together with 38 years’ experience in furniture and interior design. Kneller Design Ltd. can achieve a comprehensive and fast turnaround of projects offering individual solutions to the client’s specialist needs.

Kneller Design Office & Commercial Design

Lee started in the furniture industry back in the mid 80’s working for some of the biggest furniture manufacturers, working in the design office he was responsible for the implication of new ranges of office furniture. Lee then moved to the front line, working directly with the clients in finding solutions for the office with the escalating introduction of high tech office equipment. Being involved with the rapidly changing world of office environments for nearly 40 years Lee has gained a wealth of knowledge and understanding for the new flexible ways of working.

Working with CAD since it’s early inception and with a keen eye for the aesthetic form, his ability to find a unique solution for every situation is second to none. Working with Hunters for more than 25 years, Lee can offer guidance and advice on regulations and practice methods in planning and implementing new and exciting changes in the office. Lee has worked with many of the big corporations over the years including, ITN, The Financial Times, The New York Times, GlaxoSmithKline, Random House, Samsung, and Cancer Research. Recently Lee was a key member of Hunters/Landsec project team in finding individual solutions for each department and helping them embrace a more flexible and productive way of working. With a working knowledge of the many diverse aspects confronting the modern facilities manager, Lee is uniquely placed in bringing together the practical and creative elements that make today’s office environment.
